A rule of life names small rhythms that carry you: prayer, work, rest, study, service, community. Start simple. Review monthly. Keep what helps.

Sketch Your Rule

Prayer: Daily times and forms.

Work: Craft, commitments, boundaries.

Rest: Sabbath, sleep, delight.

Study: Scripture, tradition, philosophy.

Service: Concrete acts for specific people.

Community: How you show up and listen.
