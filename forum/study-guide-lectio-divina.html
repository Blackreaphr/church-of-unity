Study Guide: Lectio Divina

Slow reading, honest prayer, steady change

Lectio divina is slow, prayerful reading. Choose a short passage and move simply: read, meditate, pray, contemplate, and resolve. Keep each step small and honest.

Steps

1. Read

Read aloud. Mark one word or phrase that stands out.

2. Meditate

Consider what it shows about God and the human heart.

3. Pray

Speak honestly to God. Ask for wisdom and mercy.

4. Contemplate

Rest in silence for a minute or two.

5. Resolve

Name one small act of love for today.

Share

After trying it this week, add a note with one sentence about what you noticed and how it may change your day.
