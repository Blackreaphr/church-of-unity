How do I start a topic?

Open Start a Topic, copy the template, create a file under /forum/, and add it to /data/forum-posts.json.

What belongs here?

Longer reflections, questions that invite reasons, careful dialogue. Keep posts focused and kind.

How are posts moderated?

We remove harassment, personal attacks, and spam. We favor clarity, charity, and reasons offered plainly.
