Justice

Biblical anchors: Amos 5:24, Micah 6:8

Notes: Justice names God’s right ordering and faithful judgment that defends the poor.

Prayer: Teach us to love what is right and to do it with mercy.

Mercy

Biblical anchors: Psalm 136, Luke 6:36

Notes: Mercy is God’s steadfast love that heals and restores communion.

Prayer: Make our hearts resemble yours in compassion.

Wisdom

Biblical anchors: Proverbs 8, 1 Corinthians 1:24

Notes: Wisdom orders creation and guides seekers toward the good.

Prayer: Give us understanding to walk in your ways.

Word

Biblical anchors: John 1:1–14

Notes: The Word is God’s self communication. In Christian faith, the Word is Jesus Christ.

Prayer: Let your Word dwell in us and bear fruit.

Life

Biblical anchors: John 10:10, John 14:6

Notes: God gives life and calls all into fullness.

Prayer: Breathe new life where we are tired or afraid.

Death

Biblical anchors: 1 Corinthians 15:26, Romans 14:8

Notes: Death is an enemy overcome by God. Our times are in God’s hands.

Prayer: Hold us in courage as we entrust our lives to you.

Resurrection

Biblical anchors: John 11:25, 1 Corinthians 15

Notes: Resurrection is God’s victory over death and the promise of new creation.

Prayer: Raise what has fallen and make all things new.
