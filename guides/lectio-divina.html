Lectio Divina

Choose a short passage. Move slowly. 10-15 minutes.

Tip: A new suggestion appears each day. You can also choose a passage you already love.

1. Read

Read aloud. Mark one word or phrase that stands out.

2. Meditate

Consider what it shows about God and the human heart.

3. Pray

Speak honestly to God. Ask for wisdom and mercy.

4. Contemplate

Rest in silence for a minute or two.

5. Resolve

Name one small act of love for today.
