Daily Examen

Time: 5-10 minutes. Place: quiet, phone away.

1. Gratitude

Recall two or three gifts from today. Name them simply.

2. Review

Walk through the day. Notice moments of light and shadow.

3. Sorrow

Name one way you fell short in love. Ask for mercy.

4. Resolve

Choose one concrete step for tomorrow. Keep it small and clear.

Notes

End with a brief prayer. If helpful, write one sentence about what you learned.
