Overview

Christians do not confess blind fate. We confess providence. God sustains and guides creation toward the good. Destiny, in this light, is not an impersonal script. It is a vocation to become holy in Christ. Within providence, human freedom is real and carries moral weight. Our choices shape character now and bear weight in eternity.

Models in Brief

  • Classical providence: God as first cause works in and through secondary causes, including free human acts.
  • Compatibilist accounts: Some hold that a determined order and divine foreknowledge coexist with responsibility when choices arise from one’s character and reasons.
  • Open and dynamic accounts: Others hold that God knows all that can be known and engages creatures in a genuine history of response.
